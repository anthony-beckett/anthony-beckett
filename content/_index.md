I am driven by the conviction that technology should never be a cold, disembodied force, but something deeply human—shaped by ethics, society, and the primordial questions of being.

I am most skilled in C, C++, Python, and JavaScript/TypeScript. I work primarily with React and Svelte, and I am most at home in GNU/Linux and other \*NIX systems such as OpenBSD and FreeBSD. Recently, I have begun experimenting with 9 Front / Plan 9. While I work effectively in web development, my deepest interests lie in embedded and systems programming.

My passion lies at the meeting-place of systems, people, and the Good: how technology shapes our being, and how we, in turn, shape it. Outside of work, I study metaphysics and theology, read, care for animals, hike, and fish. I also hope to pursue hunting, traditional crafts, and artistic work such as sketching, painting, and writing.
